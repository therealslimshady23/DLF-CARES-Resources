🎯 DLF NCR Expansion Strategy

High-dropout districts — Hisar (24.7%), Jind (21.3%), Bulandshahr (25.3%), Meerut (22.1%) — represent critical need zones. DLF's holistic model (counselling + remedial + career guidance) directly targets the drivers of rural dropout. Expansion into Tier-2 NCR (15%+ dropout) offers 10× more impact per scholar than metro Delhi, where retention is already strong.
